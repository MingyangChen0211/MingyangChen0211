Here is **Mingyang Chen (陈铭阳)**.<br>
<br>
I am a Master student in Criminology and Criminal Justice in University of Macau. Before studying in University of Macau, I got a LL.B. degree from China University of Political Science and Law and also pass the China Bar Exam. My research interests include Sentencing, Machine Learning in Criminal Justice, and Criminal Policy Evaluation. <br>
<br>
Recently, our work got the best paper honorable mention award on AAAI2026 Bridge program. Our recent work for analyzing judge decisione behavior using NLP tools is published online on the journal Law, Probability & Risk. <br>
